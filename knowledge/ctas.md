# Call-to-Action Variants

CTAs are the bridge between engagement and conversion. These variants are tested and ranked by actual conversion performance.

## HIGH CONVERTING CTAs 🎯

### "Link in Bio" Direct
- **Text**: "[App Name] is free to try → link in bio"
- **Conversion Rate**: 0.142% (142 per 100K views)
- **Best For**: High-engagement posts where viewers are already invested
- **Performance**: Works best when content clearly shows transformation
- **Placement**: Final slide, bottom third with arrow pointing up

### App Store Search
- **Text**: "Search '[App Name]' on the App Store"
- **Conversion Rate**: 0.128% (128 per 100K views)  
- **Best For**: Posts with strong product demonstration
- **Performance**: Higher intent signals, lower friction than link clicks
- **Placement**: Final slide with app icon or screenshot visible

### Soft Discovery Angle
- **Text**: "The app I used for this (free download)"
- **Conversion Rate**: 0.115% (115 per 100K views)
- **Best For**: Educational/tutorial content where app is the tool
- **Performance**: Feels natural, not pushy
- **Placement**: Woven into content narrative, not just final slide

## CTA Placement Strategy

### Slide 6 (Primary CTA)
- **Position**: Bottom third of image, readable over any background
- **Style**: Clear, contrasting text with directional indicator
- **Context**: After transformation reveal, when viewer is convinced

### Slide 4-5 (Soft Integration)
- **Position**: Natural part of the narrative
- **Style**: Conversational mention, not obvious advertising
- **Context**: During product demonstration or process explanation

### Caption CTA (Secondary)
- **Position**: End of story caption, after value delivery
- **Style**: Matches caption tone, feels like natural recommendation
- **Context**: For users who read full captions

## Performance Correlation Patterns

### High-Converting Post Characteristics
- **Hook engagement >7%** → CTA conversion improves 40%
- **Clear transformation shown** → "Link in bio" performs best
- **Educational content style** → Soft integration CTAs work better
- **Problem-solution narrative** → Direct app search CTAs excel

### Conversion Killers
- **Multiple CTAs** → Confusion reduces conversion 60%
- **CTA too early** → Before value shown, conversions drop 80%  
- **Competing actions** → "Like + share + download" splits attention
- **Weak hook engagement** → Even best CTAs convert <0.05%

- **Extended**: 2-24 hours (15% of conversions)

### Delayed/Organic (Cross-Session)
- **Next day**: 24-48 hours (some users think it over)
- **Weekend effect**: Friday posts convert better on weekends
- **Search behavior**: Users search app name days later

## CTA Copy Best Practices

### Language That Converts
- **Specific action**: "Search [App Name]" vs "Check this out"
- **Clear value**: "Free to try" vs generic "Download"
- **Natural integration**: Part of story vs obvious advertisement
- **Confidence**: "You'll love this" vs "Hope you like it"

### Words to Avoid
- **Superlatives**: "Amazing", "incredible" (sound fake)
- **Pressure**: "Now", "hurry" (creates resistance)
- **Uncertainty**: "Maybe", "might" (reduces confidence)
- **Generic**: "Awesome", "cool" (no specific value)
