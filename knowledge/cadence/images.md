# Cadence — Image Style Guide

Visual identity for Cadence social content. Premium wellness aesthetic — calm, clean, trustworthy.

## Style Foundation

### Base Prompt Template
```
iPhone photo of [scene], clean modern aesthetic, soft natural lighting, warm neutral tones, taken on iPhone 15 Pro. Portrait orientation 1024x1536. [Consistency anchors]. No text, no watermarks, no logos.
```

### Consistency Anchors
Lock these across all slides in a single post:

**Setting:**
- Same room/environment throughout
- Same surfaces and materials
- Same personal items visible
- Same level of tidiness

**Lighting & Atmosphere:**
- Soft, diffused natural light (morning or golden hour)
- Warm color temperature
- No harsh shadows
- Airy, breathable feel

**Perspective:**
- Same camera angle and height
- Same distance from subject
- Consistent depth of field
- Same compositional balance

## Proven Style Categories

### 🏆 PRIMARY STYLES (Use Most Often)

#### Clean Wellness Lifestyle
- **Description**: Bright, organized personal spaces with health/wellness context
- **Key Elements**: Clean countertops, water bottle, morning light, phone with app visible
- **Prompt Addition**: "Clean modern bathroom or kitchen counter, morning routine setup, soft daylight through window"
- **Consistency Locks**: "White marble or light stone counter, same window placement, same personal items"
- **Cadence Context**: App open on phone, medication supplies neatly arranged, journal nearby

#### Hands-On Tracking
- **Description**: Close-up of hands interacting with phone/app
- **Key Elements**: Phone screen showing app UI, clean hands, simple background
- **Prompt Addition**: "Close-up of hands holding iPhone, soft depth-of-field background, natural skin tones"
- **Consistency Locks**: "Same hand position, same phone angle, same background blur level"
- **Cadence Context**: Show logging a dose, viewing a chart, generating a report

#### Doctor's Office Ready
- **Description**: Organized health data in a medical context
- **Key Elements**: Clean desk, printed report, tablet/phone, professional setting
- **Prompt Addition**: "Clean medical office desk, organized papers, professional calm atmosphere"
- **Consistency Locks**: "Same desk surface, same lighting direction, same level of organization"
- **Cadence Context**: Progress report printout, phone with Cadence open, professional setting

### 🧪 TESTING STYLES

#### Morning Routine Flat Lay
- **Description**: Top-down view of protocol items arranged neatly
- **Key Elements**: Medication, phone, water, supplements, clean surface
- **Prompt Addition**: "Flat lay on white surface, morning light, minimal arrangement"
- **Status**: Untested — queue for first content batch

#### Before/After Organization
- **Description**: Messy notes → clean app interface side-by-side
- **Key Elements**: Scattered papers/notes vs organized app screen
- **Prompt Addition**: "Split composition, left side messy, right side organized"
- **Status**: Untested — queue for first content batch

### ❌ AVOID THESE

#### Clinical/Medical Aesthetic
- **Problem**: Too cold, triggers medical anxiety
- **Don't**: Sterile white backgrounds, medical equipment in focus, clinical lighting

#### Biohacker/Gym Bro
- **Problem**: Wrong audience, wrong brand
- **Don't**: Dark moody lighting, supplement stacks, aggressive energy

#### Stock Photo Generic
- **Problem**: Feels fake, no personality
- **Don't**: Perfect models, overly posed, generic "wellness" imagery

#### Weight Loss Before/After
- **Problem**: Compliance risk, off-brand
- **Don't**: Body comparison shots, scale close-ups, measuring tape imagery

## Technical Specifications

### Dimensions
- **TikTok/Reels**: 1024x1536 (9:16 portrait)
- **Instagram Carousel**: 1080x1350 (4:5)
- **X/Twitter**: 1200x675 (16:9 landscape)

### Image Generation
- **Model**: Use highest quality available
- **Quality**: HD setting
- **Style**: Natural/realistic (never "vivid" or "artistic")
- **Key modifiers**: "iPhone photo", "realistic lighting", "natural colors"

### Text Overlay Zones
- **Primary text**: 20-40% from top
- **Secondary text**: 45-65% from top
- **Avoid bottom 20%**: Platform UI covers this
- **Text style**: White with subtle shadow, or dark text on light backgrounds matching Cadence brand

### Cadence Brand Colors in Imagery
When showing app UI or branded elements:
- Deep sage green (#2D5A4C) as accent
- Warm off-white (#FAFAF8) backgrounds
- Clean, airy composition
- No loud colors competing with the brand palette

## Content-Specific Image Rules

### Screen Recordings
- Use actual app or realistic mockup
- Show real-looking (but fake) health data
- Highlight the specific feature being discussed
- Clean, no notification clutter

### Lifestyle Shots
- Real-feeling environments (not studios)
- Personal touches (coffee, book, plant)
- Phone present but not dominating
- Natural, unstaged energy

### Data Visualizations
- Clean charts matching Cadence design language
- Readable at mobile size
- Use Cadence color palette
- Never show fake medical data that could be misleading
