## Hook Writing Guidelines

### Structure That Works for Cadence
1. **Personal protocol frustration** — "I've been on [medication] and [relatable problem]"
2. **Accountability moment** — Doctor visit, partner asking, looking at own data
3. **Disorganization reveal** — The messy reality of managing a protocol
4. **Discovery framing** — "Then I found..." or "There's actually an app built for..."

### GLP-1 Specific Language
- **Name the medication**: Ozempic, Mounjaro, Wegovy, semaglutide, tirzepatide — specific names outperform generic "GLP-1"
- **Reference real moments**: Injection day, dose adjustment, doctor appointments, weigh-ins
- **Acknowledge the cost**: People paying $500-1500/month are motivated to track properly
- **Never imply medical outcomes**: Track and organize, don't promise results

### Emotional Triggers (On-Brand)
- Frustration with disorganization (not fear of failure)
- Relief when things click into place (not hype about transformation)
- Pride in showing up prepared (not shame about falling behind)
- Curiosity about own patterns (not anxiety about progress)
