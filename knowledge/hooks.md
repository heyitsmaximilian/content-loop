## Hook Writing Guidelines

### Structure That Works
1. **Immediate conflict/tension** - "My landlord said I can't paint"
2. **Relatable frustration** - "Our apartment looks so boring" 
3. **Unexpected solution** - "Then I found this app"
4. **Visual transformation promise** - "The results are incredible"

### Language Patterns
- **Use "I" statements** → Personal experience feels authentic
- **Include emotional triggers** → Frustration, excitement, surprise
- **Create curiosity gaps** → "You won't believe what happened next"
- **Avoid superlatives** → "Amazing" and "incredible" feel fake

### Performance Indicators
- **Views** → Did it stop the scroll?
- **Comments** → Did it start conversations? 
- **Shares** → Did people want to show others?
- **Conversions** → Did it drive downloads?
